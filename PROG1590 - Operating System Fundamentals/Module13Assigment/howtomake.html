Step 1: Gather Materials

You will need:

  • Bread
  • Toaster

NOTE: If your bread has not yet been cut into slices, you must do so before continuing.

Step 2: Toast Bread

  1. Make sure your toaster is plugged in to a wall outlet.
  2. Place one or two slices of toast into the toaster.
  3. Push down the toast plunger.
  4. Wait for toaster to reach completion
  5. When toasting is done, remove former bread from toaster and place onto a plate

WARNING: Do not pull toast out of toaster immediately. During the toasting process, the bread gets very warm and will burn you if you do not give it time to cool.

Step 3: Condiments

Now's your chance to cover your toast in whatever you like!

Popular choices include:

  • Butter
  • Margarine
  • Vegemite

Step 4: Eat!

This part is up to you. Good luck!
